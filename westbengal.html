Sandakphu

The Sandakphu trek is the highest peak in West Bengal, located in the Singalila National Park in the Darjeeling district. It offers stunning panoramic views of four of the world's tallest peaks: Mount Everest, Kanchenjunga, Lhotse, and Makalu. The trek takes you through rhododendron forests, beautiful alpine meadows, and charming villages.

Weather: -5°C to 15°C

Best Time to Visit: April-May & October-December

Tabakoshi

Tabakoshi is a scenic village near Mirik, West Bengal, nestled amidst lush tea gardens. It is known for its breathtaking landscapes, the Balasun River, and serene ambiance. The village offers an ideal retreat for nature lovers and travelers looking for peace away from crowded tourist spots.

Weather: 10°C to 25°C

Best Time to Visit: Throughout the year
